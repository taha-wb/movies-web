synopsis

The series is based on a boy and an alien parasitic creature whom fate has ordained to live symbiotically with the boy. But not all of them are symbiotic. Thus, the future of humanity is in danger. But there's more. It shows how a parasite changes a host, upto a point where the boy almost loses his humanity. And in the end we shall know who the real parasite is - Us!!!
